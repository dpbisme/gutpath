GutPath is a collaborative project from the [Mucosal Immunology Study Team (MIST) Consortium](https://mucosal.org/), a interdisciplinary group of scientists focused on immune defense mechanisms and immune regulation at mucosal surfaces.  The over arching goal of the MIST program is to discover and define novel basic immune mechanisms, cells, mediators, and pathways that underly mucosal immune defense.

The GutPath Atlas helps to advance this mission by providing researchers with convenient access to a massive collection of single cell data from the small intestine across a wide range of well-controlled infection expeirments.  All
