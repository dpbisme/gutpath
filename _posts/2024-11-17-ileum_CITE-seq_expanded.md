This dataset contains over 450,000 cells from the epithelial layer and laminia propria of the distal small intestine of C57BL6 mice.  In addition, some cells were recovered from the duodenum.  All mice were derived from the same commercial provider (Jackson Laboratories) and the same breeding room.  

**[Download the full dataset](https://penno365-my.sharepoint.com/:u:/g/personal/beiting_upenn_edu/IQD82JALtfgsT5QrNRdDD3AKAQ0qcsKsO4QJpI88f78tDC4?e=2Y2990)** as a Seurat object, complete with annotations.
