This Xenium spatial transcriptomics dataset includes over 1.7 million cells in total from from the small intestine of 9 mice across spanning 5 different conditions (WT naive, Germ-free naive, and WT mice infected with *Yersinia pseudotuberculosis*, *Cryptosporidum parvum*, or *Nippostrongylus brasiliensis*).  All tissues were prepared as swiss rolls.  

Clicking the button below will lauch a custom instance of CELLxGENE.  *Note cell numbers have not been downsampled.*
