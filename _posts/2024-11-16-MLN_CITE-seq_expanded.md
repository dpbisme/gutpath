This dataset contains over 260,000 cells from the messenteric lymph node (MLN) that drains the distal small intestine.  All mice were derived from the same commercial provider (Jackson Laboratories) and the same breeding room.  

**[Download the full dataset](https://penno365-my.sharepoint.com/:u:/g/personal/beiting_upenn_edu/IQCvKqvTA_tlTZ0f5mn_CHsuAVwE19EBcR6hzlTw84J1ho4?e=PKQvxU)** as a Seurat object, complete with annotations.
